About Me

Failed startup founder turned Software Engineer. I learned to tackle difficult problems by breaking them down into smaller more manageable moving parts and in return I mastered how I learn altogether.

I studied film/audio and interned for a prominent Post-Production agency during my college years. I’ve always been around technology but it wasn’t until I co-founded a company and worked side by side with developers when I realized I should be in their seats not my current one (as a co-founder). I found myself trying to do the work of the developers and almost ruined the business relationship, however I was obsessed! After we closed the doors on the startup I began self teaching myself Python.
After a few months of self teaching I landed a full time position at a tech startup where I was promoted 3 times before landing my current position(s).
I enrolled in Lambda School and 18 months later here I am, building full stack web applications!
